SaltWorks is an automation puzzle game set in a potash mine. Engineer extraction, haulage, and processing across a two-level grid — while self-closing galleries decay your layout, one fuse at a time.

01The mechanic

Every cell is on a fuse.

Closure

Nothing is permanent

A cell stays dormant until material first flows through it — then its fuse lights and runs one-way to collapse. Route around your own decay.

Two layers

Volatile over stable

Mine the volatile production layer, drop ore through author-placed passes to the stable infrastructure below. Plan in three dimensions.

Bolting

Buy time, pay in time

Rock-bolt a cell to stop it decaying — but installs are serialized by a single crew, so every bolt costs you ticks. Spend them wisely.
